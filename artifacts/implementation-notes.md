## Cross-Platform Consistency

Use the same one-line pitch across X bio, newsletter footer, and cold DMs. Brand recognition compounds when the message doesn't change.

**Fiverr pitch:** "I build AI systems that turn your 40-hour workweek into a 3-hour operation, without hiring a team."

**Upwork pitch:** "I engineer scripts and copy that turn scrollers into subscribers, and subscribers into buyers."

These are two different service lines (AI/automation vs. scripts/copy). Pick ONE as your primary cross-platform identity unless running both simultaneously as separate income streams.

## $27 Book Bundle Integration

Add as P.S. in both Upwork proposals and Fiverr gig descriptions:
"P.S. Want the full system? Grab my $27 book bundle at [link]."

This converts profile views into revenue even before a hire happens.
